Frequently Asked Questions

What are the opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on weekends.

How can I contact you?

You can reach us by phone at +32(0)33773235, by email at info@groentenboers.be, or via the contact form on our website.

What is the delivery time for orders?

Standard orders are delivered within 3 to 5 business days. For urgent orders, we offer an express option with delivery within 24 hours.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order free of charge up to 24 hours after placement. Please contact our customer service for this.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), Bancontact, PayPal, and bank transfer. All payments are processed securely.
