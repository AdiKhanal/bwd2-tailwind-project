About us

What do you get when you combine a passion for honest food with a long-standing friendship? The answer is De Groentenboers.

Who are De Groentenboers?

Pieter is the face of our selection. With his open and friendly appearance, he heads out daily to find the very best vegetables and fruit. For Pieter, only the highest quality is good enough; he maintains close ties with our local farmers and ensures that only the freshest harvest ends up in our crates. Ari is the organizational brain behind the webshop. Thanks to his sharp eye for detail and passion for logistics, the online ordering process runs flawlessly. Ari ensures that Pieter's carefully selected products are delivered to your front door at the right time and in perfect condition.

Our Mission

What started as a shared dream at the local market has grown into the online destination for everyone who loves fresh, healthy, and local. At De Groentenboers, we believe in the short chain: fewer kilometers, more flavor. We get up early every day to guarantee the freshness that you often miss in the supermarket. With us, you don't just buy vegetables; you support a vision of sustainability and craftsmanship. We are Pieter and Ari, and we look forward to bringing the tastiest products from the land into your kitchen. Welcome to De Groentenboers!
